Biography

In 2008 I moved from my hometown, Valparaiso, Chile to Seattle. As a student in statistics I saw opportunities for growth here however I did not yet speak English. I found work in kitchens and moved my way up from dishwasher to a lead line-cook over 9 years with Tom Douglas Restaurants. When I was able to study and take classes in English I immediately entered the web development program at Seattle Central. The skills I gained in kitchens quickly proved useful in many aspects of the program and my career path. It had always been a goal to enter to this industry and I have enjoyed expanding my knowledge of full stack development. After completing this program I look forward to building my career as a web developer in a challenging and impactful work environment.
